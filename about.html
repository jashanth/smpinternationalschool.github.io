About Us

About SMP International School

Conscious of the fact that life has never been so diverse, today’s education needs to nurture the students to evolve independently, encourage them to have an international outlook and instil a healthy spirit of competition in order that they learn and understand the responsibility that success brings with it. SMP International School is providing students with a leading edge in the journey of life…so that they acquire the wings of knowledge to soar higher into the infinite skies of opportunities on their own as they prepare to leave our portal.With years of dedicated research done by its Child Development Experts, SMP International High School has developed the best-in-class child centric learning methodology

Faculty

The Principal, as head of the campus must take responsibility in making the staff - teaching and administrative - competent, by conducting training programmes periodically. The teaching staff are exposed to innovative academic programmes by the Directors and evolve competent skills in delivering the same effectively in the classrooms. Orientation programmes are conducted for the administrative staff to enable them to cope with their responsibilities better. Training sessions are conducted during holidays / vacations, through workshops on various methodologies. We also groom Junior Lecturers to take up teaching tasks.

Extra-Curricular

Since physical growth is as vital as mental advancement, students are inspired and motivated to put their individual skills to the test in a wide range of physical sports. Apart from its key benefit of endurance and stamina, coordination, and balance, within the wider context, these activities teach team spirit, self-confidence, a sense of competitive spirit and discipline as well. We do have finest facilities to help students to achieve their full potential, we also have a committed squad of coaches, which is ably supported by a number of highly qualified assistant instructors.

Academics

SMP International High School follows an eclectic approach to curriculum with the Andhra Pradesh State Board of Secondary Education (SSC) syllabus and CBSC syllabus with self-developed initiatives to enrich the educational method. It follows SSC syllabus CBSC syllabus with a view to bring under its umbrella the wide majority of students. The modern class rooms provide an atmosphere conducive to learning. The school fully leverages the latest audiovisual teaching aids and computer system in each class for enhancing the educational experience. Apart from curriculum learning, a wide selection of sporting, cultural and performing arts activities is also offered to students for a well-rounded personality.
